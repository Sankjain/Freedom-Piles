How The Device Works?

Freeze

Place the CRYOCURETM in the freezing compartment of the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.

Apply

Take the CRYOCURETM out of the freezer and apply some Vaseline (White Petroleum Jelly) on the stick and around the anus.

Relax

Lie on your side and insert the thinner portion of the CRYOCURETM stick into the anus (up until the broader side of the stick).
Let the CRYOCURETM stick remain inside the anus for just about ten minutes.

Remove

Remove the CRYOCURETM stick from the anus and clean it with water and soap.
Put the CRYOCURETM stick back inside the caseand leave it in the freezing compartment of the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
